News

New texts will be added in the Texts section of this website in course of the project.

The first texts that will appear here are the Middle Welsh tales of the Mabinogion and the 16th century Llyfr Ancr texts with corrected annotations by Elena Parina & Raphael Sackmann.

If you use our corpus, please cite us.
